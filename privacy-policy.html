Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the Coding Pet Extension ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

1. Usage Data

  • LeetCode problem-solving progress
  • Daily coding goals and achievements
  • Extension usage patterns
  • Error reports and performance data

2. Technical Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Extension version
  • Error logs and crash reports

3. User Feedback

  • Feedback submissions
  • Feature requests
  • Bug reports

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the extension's functionality
  • To track and display your coding progress
  • To improve the extension's performance and user experience
  • To respond to your feedback and support requests
  • To send important updates and notifications

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not store your personal data on our servers unless you explicitly submit feedback or error reports.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
